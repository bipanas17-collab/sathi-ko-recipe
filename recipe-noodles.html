Dietary: Vegetarian

Fast, healthy noodles with colorful vegetables. A quick fix for students looking for something delicious in under 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 packet instant noodles
  • 1/2 cup carrots, julienned
  • 1/2 cup capsicum, sliced
  • 1/4 cup cabbage, shredded
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp chili sauce (optional)
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil Noodles: Cook noodles as per packet instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Stir Fry Veggies: Heat oil in a pan, add carrots, capsicum, and cabbage. Stir fry for 2‚Äì3 minutes.
  3. Combine: Add cooked noodles, soy sauce, chili sauce, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  4. Serve: Enjoy hot with a squeeze of lime or chili flakes.

Tips & Variations

  • Add scrambled egg or paneer for extra protein.
  • Use frozen mixed vegetables for a quicker version.
  • Upgrade with sesame oil for authentic flavor.
