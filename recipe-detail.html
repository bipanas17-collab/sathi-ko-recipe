Momo (Steamed Dumplings)

Dietary: Vegetarian, Vegan option

Fluffy steamed dumplings with a simple vegetable filling, served with dipping sauce. A perfect comfort food for students on a budget.

‚òÖ ‚òÖ ‚òÖ ‚òÖ ‚òÜ (4.5/5)

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup finely chopped cabbage
  • 1 cup grated carrot
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp garlic paste
  • 1 tsp ginger paste
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Water (for dough)

Instructions

  1. Prepare Dough: Mix flour with water and a pinch of salt. Knead into soft dough. Rest for 20 minutes.
  2. Prepare Filling: Combine cabbage, carrot, onion, soy sauce, garlic, ginger, salt, and pepper.
  3. Shape Momo: Roll dough into small circles, add filling, and fold into dumpling shapes.
  4. Steam: Place momo in a steamer lined with parchment or oil. Steam for 10‚Äì12 minutes until cooked.
  5. Serve: Enjoy hot with tomato achar (sauce).

Tips & Variations

  • Add minced chicken for a non-veg option.
  • Experiment with dipping sauces (spicy tomato, sesame, or peanut sauce).
  • Make extra and freeze for later ‚Äî saves time for busy students.
