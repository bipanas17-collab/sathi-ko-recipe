Dietary: Vegan, Gluten-free option

Quick and flavorful stir-fry with crispy tofu and seasonal vegetables tossed in a spicy sauce. A protein-packed meal for students on the go.

Ingredients

  • 250g firm tofu, cubed
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce (or tamari for GF)
  • 1 tbsp chili sauce or sriracha
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1 tsp cornstarch (optional, for sauce thickness)
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil (for frying)

Instructions

  1. Prepare Tofu: Pat tofu dry and pan-fry in oil until golden and crisp on all sides.
  2. Saut√© Aromatics: Heat sesame oil, add garlic and ginger. Stir until fragrant.
  3. Add Veggies: Toss in broccoli, bell pepper, and carrot. Stir-fry for 4‚Äì5 minutes.
  4. Make Sauce: Mix soy sauce, chili sauce, and cornstarch with 2 tbsp water.
  5. Combine: Add tofu back into the pan, pour sauce, and toss until evenly coated.
  6. Serve: Garnish with sesame seeds or scallions. Enjoy with rice or noodles.

Tips & Variations

  • Swap tofu with tempeh for variety.
  • Add extra chili flakes if you like it spicier.
  • Serve over quinoa or brown rice for a healthier option.
