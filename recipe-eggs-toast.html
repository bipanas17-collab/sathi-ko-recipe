A quick and classic breakfast option ‚Äî toasted bread topped with fried or scrambled eggs. Perfect for busy students who want a nutritious start to the day.

‚òÖ ‚òÖ ‚òÖ ‚òÜ ‚òÜ (3.5/5)

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp butter or oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: sliced avocado, tomato, or cheese

Instructions

  1. Toast Bread: Lightly toast the bread slices until golden brown.
  2. Cook Eggs: Heat butter or oil in a pan. Fry or scramble the eggs to your preference.
  3. Assemble: Place eggs on the toast, sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  4. Optional: Add avocado, tomato slices, or cheese for extra flavor.
  5. Serve: Enjoy hot with a cup of tea or coffee.

Tips & Variations

  • Use whole grain bread for a healthier option.
  • Add chili flakes for a spicy kick.
  • Turn it into a sandwich by adding extra veggies and cheese.
