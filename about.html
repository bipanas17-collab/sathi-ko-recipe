About Us

Our story, mission, and the team behind Sathi Ko Recipe

Our Mission

Sathi Ko Recipe was created to support international students and beginner cooks who often struggle with limited time, small budgets, and little cooking experience. Instead of turning to fast food, our platform helps them prepare affordable, healthy, and easy-to-follow meals.

By focusing on clarity, accessibility, and inclusivity, we aim to make cooking less intimidating and more enjoyable — even for first-timers.

Background & Purpose

This project was built as part of a Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) study, applying Shneiderman’s Eight Golden Rules to ensure usability and a positive user experience. From planning and wireframing to testing, every step focused on making the website easy to navigate and valuable for its users.

We believe that everyone deserves access to healthy, simple meals, no matter where they live or study.
