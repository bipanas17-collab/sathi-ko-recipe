Nutritious rice and lentils served with seasonal vegetables and achar. A staple Nepali meal that is budget-friendly and filling.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1 cup split lentils (moong or masoor)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook Rice: Wash rice and cook in a rice cooker or pot until fluffy.
  2. Prepare Lentils: Boil lentils with turmeric and salt until soft.
  3. Make Tadka: In a pan, heat oil, add cumin seeds, onion, garlic, and tomatoes. Cook until aromatic.
  4. Mix: Add boiled lentils into the tadka. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Serve: Plate rice with dal, pickles, and saut√©ed greens.
