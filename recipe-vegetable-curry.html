Vegetable Curry

‚è± 40 minutes ‚Ä¢ üçΩ Serves 4 ‚Ä¢ üí∞ Budget-friendly

Dietary: Vegetarian, Vegan option

A wholesome mixed vegetable curry packed with spices and flavor. Perfect for weeknight dinners served with rice or roti.

‚òÖ ‚òÖ ‚òÖ ‚òÖ ‚òÜ (4/5)

Ingredients

  • 2 medium potatoes, diced
  • 1 cup cauliflower florets
  • 1 cup green beans, chopped
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp chili powder (optional)
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Saut√© Base: Heat oil, add cumin seeds and onion. Fry until golden.
  2. Add Tomatoes: Stir in tomatoes and cook until soft.
  3. Add Spices: Mix in turmeric, coriander, and chili powder.
  4. Add Vegetables: Add potatoes, beans, carrots, and cauliflower. Stir well.
  5. Simmer: Add 1 cup water, cover, and cook for 20 minutes until veggies are tender.
  6. Finish: Sprinkle garam masala, adjust salt, and serve hot with rice or roti.

Tips & Variations

  • Swap in seasonal vegetables for variety.
  • Add coconut milk for a creamy South Asian twist.
  • For spicier curry, increase chili powder or add green chilies.
